Privacy Policy

Ancestrio is built to keep family history private by default. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the controls you have.

Last updated: March 5, 2026 Applies to: Ancestrio web app

What we collect

We only collect what is needed to run the family workspace and fulfill keepsake requests.

  • Account details such as email, username, and display name.
  • Family tree data you enter (names, relationships, birthdays, places, and optional photos).
  • Order request data (contact details, shipping address, and any notes you submit).
  • Local preferences stored in your browser (theme choice, consent settings, guest drafts).

How we use your data

Your information is used to provide the workspace, render the tree views, and follow up on keepsake requests.

Workspace functionality.

We store your tree data so you can access it across sessions and devices.

Keepsake fulfillment.

Order requests are used to confirm details and produce the requested items.

Where data is stored

Ancestrio uses Firebase Authentication and Firestore (Google Cloud) to store account and tree data. We do not sell or trade family information.

Guest mode keeps data in your browser only until you sign in.

Your controls

You can delete your account from the dashboard at any time. Deleting your account removes your trees, orders, and profile data.
