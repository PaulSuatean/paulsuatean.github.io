Cookies & Local Storage

Ancestrio keeps tracking minimal. This page explains the small amount of storage and optional widgets used on the site.

Last updated: March 5, 2026 Applies to: Ancestrio web app

Necessary storage

We use local storage to keep the site working:

  • Session and sign-in state for the dashboard.
  • Theme preference (light or dark).
  • Guest mode drafts saved locally in your browser.

Optional widgets

The Buy Me a Coffee widget is optional and only loads after you consent. If you decline, the widget does not run.

We do not run third-party advertising or analytics scripts on this site today.

Manage consent

On pages where optional widgets are available, you can change consent using the Cookie Buddy button in the lower corner.
